Maybe

Poem
Maybe, if I had known you better Maybe, if I had known that you wanted love differently Maybe, if I was educated enough Maybe, if God had wanted Maybe, if he had chosen you to come closer Maybe, if he had chosen me to part away Maybe, if you wanted the same love that I could give Maybe, if I could give the same love you wanted Maybe, if I had listened that day Maybe, if you had spoken that day Maybe, if he was there that day Maybe, if he hadn't been there that day Maybe, things might have turned out differently Maybe, I didn't had to grieve your loss Maybe, you didn't had to preserve your independence Maybe, both of us could love for what we are Maybe, if I wasn't dying to get closer Maybe, if you weren't afraid of being engulfed Maybe, if I had loved you from the window Maybe, if you had loved me at the stop
